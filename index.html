Dear Internet,

I have to say I never really used the internet until this past year.

I had used social media. You know instagram, twitter, youtube, chatgpt, whatever. And I had done programming before, but only on my local machine. I never realized how fulfilling, but also how easy it would be to put a website on the internet.

I'm not here to flex my websites or anything... honestly claude deserves more credit than me for coding them up... but. The internet (or the web) is genuinely the best place to create and share stuff. it's honestly crazy that our whole generation was psyopped into thinking that the only way to share literally anything was on closed platforms...

I've always wanted to be an artist. So I just want to say I appreciate you, "The Internet." You are the one canvas that I'm not that bad at painting on.


Ron Kiehn
